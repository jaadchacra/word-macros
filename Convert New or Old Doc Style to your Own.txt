1- Open Styles Pane (arrow under the headings)
2- Manage Styles Button (with checkmark) 
3- Import Button
4- Highlight all styles on the right side (normal.dotm)
5- Click Copy to left button
6- Override

Don't forget to change all the Margins to 0.5''
